About Me

Hello! I'm Sushil Poudel, an illustrator and web designer based in Pokhara, Nepal. I specialize in creating digital watercolor art and designing websites that reflect the same artistic sensibility.

My Journey

My creative journey began when I was studying fine arts at Kathmandu University. I discovered the beautiful blend of traditional watercolor techniques and digital tools, which allowed me to create unique artworks that maintain the organic feel of traditional media while leveraging the flexibility of digital platforms.

My Approach

I believe that design should be both beautiful and functional. Whether I'm creating an illustration or designing a website, I focus on creating work that communicates effectively while providing an aesthetic experience that resonates with the viewer.
